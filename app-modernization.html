Delivery Motion

A staged modernization rhythm that keeps risk visible.

01

Discover

Audit the current state, map dependencies, surface blockers, and define the value case for change.

02

Architect

Design the target state, choose the modernization route, and stage the migration path around business constraints.

03

Modernize

Refactor code, migrate services and data, automate releases, and validate experience improvements incrementally.

04

Stabilize
