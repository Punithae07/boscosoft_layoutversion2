CORE MODULES

Microfinance Management Platform

Explore the core MicroFUND capabilities for loan origination, portfolio monitoring, reporting, borrower communication, and community-focused financial services.

Loan Origination
Process applications efficiently from origination to approval and manage the full loan lifecycle from issuance through payoff.
Loan Portfolio Management
Monitor portfolio performance, track repayments, review trends, and support strategic decisions with clear operational visibility.
Auto-Payment Reminders
Send automated, personalized reminders by preferred channels to reduce missed payments and improve collection discipline.
Transaction History Tracking
Maintain complete records of loan disbursements, repayments, accruals, and related financial activity for transparency and accountability.
Integrated Account Management
Centralize borrower profiles, loan details, repayment schedules, statements, and support access in one connected view.
Low-Cost Loan Management
Give smaller institutions and self-help groups a budget-friendly way to manage loans without heavy infrastructure or overhead.
Financial Reporting
Generate reports on portfolio health, repayment trends, cash flow, delinquency, and borrower activity for better decisions.
Impact Data & Reporting
Track social and economic outcomes such as job creation, business growth, and community development linked to lending activity.
Customer Relationship Management
Manage interactions with current and potential clients, organize communication, and strengthen business relationships over time.
Mobile Banking Integration
Support payment collection with mobile banking and connect transaction flows to field-friendly financial operations.
SMS & Communication Tools
Use SMS and connected communication channels to keep borrowers informed and improve outreach, reminders, and support.
Peer-to-Peer Lending Support
Enable funding models that serve borrowers who may be excluded by traditional lending pathways.
Community Investment Tools
Support lending models that reinvest into communities and create pathways for local growth and financial inclusion.
Risk Assessment Support
Use reporting and portfolio insights to identify risk patterns and improve strategic adaptation across lending operations.
Mentorship-Ready Workflows

Common questions about
MicroFUND

Find answers to common questions about MicroFUND implementation and features. Discover how the platform supports community-owned financial institutions, borrowers, and lending teams.

MicroFUND is a platform that helps self-reliant, community-owned financial institutions offer essential financial services, including microloans, to individuals and small businesses through accessible, transparent, and efficient workflows.

MicroFUND is especially useful for self-employed individuals, small business owners, startups with only a few employees, minorities, women, veterans, young entrepreneurs, and freelancers who may not qualify for conventional funding options.

MicroFUND supports customer relationship management, loan origination, loan portfolio management, reporting and analytics, auto-payment reminders, transaction tracking, and borrower account management in one connected system.

MicroFUND integrates mobile banking for payment collection, supports SMS-based transaction communication, and connects with digital channels that help teams collaborate and support borrowers more effectively.

The platform is designed to lower operational friction for smaller lenders while helping them support underserved borrowers with better communication, reporting, and community-focused financing workflows.
