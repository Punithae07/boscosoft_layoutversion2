FEATURES

Comprehensive Accounting & Financial Management

Explore our integrated suite of accounting modules designed to streamline financial operations for nonprofits, NGOs, and religious institutions.

General Ledger
Complete chart of accounts management with full tracking of income, expenses, assets, and liabilities. Simplified entry processes with multi-currency support for international organizations.
Accounts Payable
Manage vendor invoices, payment schedules, and expense tracking. Streamlined approval workflows and automatic reminders for payment due dates.
Accounts Receivable
Track donations, grants, and member contributions. Automated donor receipts, tax reporting, and contribution tracking for compliance.
Fund Management
Manage restricted and unrestricted funds separately. Track fund performance and ensure donors their contributions are used as intended.
Financial Reporting
Generate comprehensive financial statements including balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow reports for regulatory compliance.
Program Tracking
Allocate and track expenses by program or mission to demonstrate impact and efficiency to stakeholders and donors.
Compliance & Audit
Built-in audit trails, approval workflows, and documentation supporting regulatory requirements for nonprofits and NGOs.
Project Accounting
Track project budgets, expenses, and revenues separately for grant-funded initiatives and impact measurement.
Budget Management
Create, monitor, and manage organizational budgets against actual spending with variance analysis and forecasting tools.
Expense Management
Streamlined expense tracking, mileage reimbursement, and approval workflows for field staff and volunteers.
Bank Reconciliation
Automated matching of bank transactions with accounting records. Easy reconciliation and exception reporting for all bank accounts.
Multi-Entity Accounting
Support for multiple legal entities, chapters, or branches with consolidated reporting and inter-company transactions.
Mobile Field Accounting
Access accounting functions on mobile devices. Capture receipts, submit expenses, and view reports offline with syncing.
Integration & APIs
Connect with banking systems, payment processors, and third-party apps for seamless data flow and automated reconciliation.
Customization Suite
Flexible template-based customization to adapt Acme.erp to your specific accounting needs and organizational structure.
