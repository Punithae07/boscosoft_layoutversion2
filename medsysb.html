Common questions about
MedSysB

Find answers to common questions about MedSysB implementation and features. Discover how MedSysB can transform your hospital workflows securely and efficiently.

MedSysB hospital management software (HMS), is a comprehensive hospital management solution designed to manage and workflow various hospital operations and processes covering patients, doctors, bills, lab tests, hospital inventory, blood bank, dietary and medical records with configurable activity-mapping and roles & rights mapping for each user of the system. Ease-of-use and intuitive-use are basic design principles of this software.

Benefits include improved patient care, enhanced efficiency, better data management, streamlined workflows, reduced administrative costs and compliance with healthcare regulations.

  • Flexible for all healthcare setups: Suitable for small and medium-scale facilities, with customized departmental and activity mapping; supports both centralized and decentralized billing systems.
  • User-friendly: Features multiple links within a single registration page (New, Renew, & Revisit), multi-departmental access and shortcut keys for ease of use.
  • Reduced patient waiting time: Enables easy patient searches, quick registration and bill preparation in seconds.
  • Advanced search options: Allows searching by patient name, payment details, bed occupancy and admission/discharge details.
  • Simple configuration: The application only needs to be installed on the server; users can access it through a URL and they can manage the build independently.

Yes, it is highly customizable to meet the specific needs of the hospital, including fee structures, lab tests, Management Information System (MIS) reports, medical certificates and case sheets with the least disruption to the live system.

Patient data is secured with encryption and pass-key protection. Biometrics and end-point protection is in the roadmap of the product.
