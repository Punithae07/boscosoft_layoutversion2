CORE MODULES

CristO Product Suite for Church Administration

Explore the four core CristO solutions and the shared ministry tools that support parishes, dioceses, religious institutes, apostolic centers, families, and individual members.

Parokia
Parish management software that helps teams organize family and parish information, streamline daily administration, and serve parish communities more effectively.
Religio
Province management software for religious institutes with communication tools, member records, assignment history, planning, reporting, and archiving.
Cathedra
Diocese management software that brings together family records, member details, parish information, communication, planning, reporting, and archives in one place.
Charisma
Denominational church management software built for community engagement, youth groups, ministry coordination, and church event administration.
Family & Member Records
Maintain church member databases, family records, sacramental data, and personal profiles with quick, organized access for ministry teams.
Communication Hub
Coordinate circulars, emails, meetings, and institutional communication across provinces, dioceses, parishes, and apostolic organizations.
Planning & Reporting
Support pastoral planning, institutional follow-up, and decision-making with structured reports, summaries, and administrative oversight tools.
Archives & History
Preserve chronicles, concerns, institutional history, and important administrative records in a searchable digital archive.
